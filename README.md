# TikTokManga 📱📖

**TikTokManga** is a next-generation manga reading application that brings the addictive, seamless vertical-swipe experience of short-form video platforms to the world of manga and manhwa. 

## 🌟 Overview

Traditional manga readers often feel clunky or static. **TikTokManga** reimagines the reading experience for mobile-first users, featuring a high-refresh-rate vertical scroll that makes transitioning between pages as fluid as browsing a social media feed.

## ✨ Key Features

*   **TikTok-Style Vertical Scroll**: Smooth, gesture-based navigation optimized for one-handed reading.
*   **Immersive Full-Screen Mode**: Focus entirely on the art with a distraction-free UI that hides status bars and menus.
*   **Smart Pre-fetching**: Background loading of upcoming pages to ensure zero-lag transitions during fast swipes.
*   **Adaptive Layout**: Supports both single-page and long-strip (Webtoon) formats automatically.
*   **Favorites & Sync**: Save your favorite titles and sync your reading progress across devices.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

*   **Frontend**: [React Native / Flutter / React.js] — *Optimized for touch gestures.*
*   **Animations**: [Framer Motion / Reanimated] — *Powering the fluid swipe transitions.*
*   **State Management**: [Redux / Zustand] — *Handling rapid page updates and caching.*
*   **API**: [MangaDex / Jikan / Custom Scraper] — *Sourcing high-quality manga data.*
